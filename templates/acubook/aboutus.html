Our Vision

To cater to the reading needs of everyone in Ajayi Crowther University and the society in general.

Our Mission

Making books available and affordable for everyone to read.

What We Do

ACU Bookstore is a unique book store that caters for the needs of Ajayi Crowther University and the surrounding oyo community. It is a one-stop shop where book lovers, those seeking enlightenment, and others looking for a ‘third-place’ can visit again and again. ACU Bookstore is owned by Ajayi Crowther University. We sell a wide range of books covering; Business, Science, Academics, Christian, Motivational and so much more.
